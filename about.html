About Arthur Menken

Hallo! I'm Arthur

Aspiring UX Designer from Holland

As a UX designer, I've always been fascinated by the digital world of websites, games, and apps. From a young age, I experimented with website builders and Photoshop CS2, and even designed levels for games like Counter-Strike. These experiences sparked my passion for creating intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences.

As a recent graduate of Communication & Multimedia Design, I have developed a strong skill set in solving complex problems using research, data analysis, and pixel-perfect design. Whether it's crafting a seamless user journey or designing a beautiful interface, I am passionate about creating meaningful experiences that delight users.

When I'm not behind a screen, I love immersing myself in games and TV series with compelling narratives and rich worlds. And when I'm feeling adventurous in the kitchen, you'll often find me whipping up Tex-Mex dishes that blend bold flavors and textures.
