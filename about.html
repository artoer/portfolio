About Arthur Menken

Hallo! I'm Arthur

Aspiring UX Designer from Holland

Ever since I could sit behind a desktop I found myself fascinated by the digital world of websites, games and apps. I experimented a lot and challenged my ingenuity. From playing with so-called website builders and Photoshop CS2 to designing levels for games such as Counter-Strike.

Nowadays I'm in my fourth year of Communication & Multimedia Design. I like solving challenging problems by using research, hard data and the pushing of lots and lots of pixels.

In my free time I like to enjoy games and TV series with good stories and immersive worlds. I also like to experiment in the kitchen when I'm not behind a screen, I am especially fond of Tex-Mex.
