About Me

I've had the pleasure to hone my skills and versatility during internships at Tweakers and Talpa eCommerce, two major players in Dutch tech media and ecommerce respectively, as well as graduating with honors in Communication and Multimedia Design. These experiences solidified my passion for impactful user-centered design.

Other passions include cooking, keeping up and tinkering with tech, getting lost in video games and TV shows, savoring good food, exploring new places, and enjoying Formula 1 and soccer.
