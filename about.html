As a UX designer, I have a passion for creating intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences. I am skilled in conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and designing engaging interactions and interfaces that align with user needs. My technical knowledge allows me to develop comprehensive handover packages and work collaboratively with developers to bring designs to life. I am dedicated to crafting seamless user journeys and designing beautiful interfaces that make a difference.

When I'm not behind a screen, I love immersing myself in games and TV series with compelling narratives and rich worlds. And when I'm feeling adventurous in the kitchen, you'll often find me whipping up Tex-Mex dishes.
