About Arthur Menken

Hallo! I'm Arthur

Aspiring UX Designer from Holland

As a UX designer, I've always been fascinated by the digital world of websites, games, and apps. From a young age, I experimented with website builders and Photoshop CS2, and even designed levels for games like Counter-Strike. These experiences ignited my interest in the field of UX design and drove me to pursue it as a career.

Creating intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences is my passion. I am skilled in conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and designing engaging interactions and interfaces that align with user needs. My technical knowledge allows me to develop lifelike prototypes and tests and work collaboratively with developers to bring designs to life. I am dedicated to crafting seamless user journeys and designing beautiful interfaces that make an impact.

When I'm not behind a screen, I love immersing myself in games and TV series with compelling narratives and rich worlds. And when I'm feeling adventurous in the kitchen, you'll often find me whipping up Tex-Mex dishes.
