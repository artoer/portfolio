A more transparent way to find jobs

Problem

During my job hunt it became apparent that jobs aren't what they seem at first glance.

Job boards incorrectly categorize jobs by experience level, intentional or not. Not only is this counterproductive, but it also muddies the water with unsuitable listings, only adding to the frustation.

It's not just me

Redditors looking for jobs have shared similar complaints, and continue to do so.

Job Story

When I'm scanning a job board website, looking for jobs I want to be able to see the real experience required, so I can find fitting jobs for me at a glance.
